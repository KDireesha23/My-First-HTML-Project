Nenu Sailaja Movie Story Information The story of Nenu Sailaja revolves around the character of Hari, a DJ in Vizag who has a history of proposing to every girl he meets but is always turned down.
         His life takes a turn when he encounters Sailaja, a young woman who seems to have a crush on him. 
         Despite his initial hesitation to express his feelings, Hari eventually confesses his love, only to be rejected. 
         Heartbroken, he moves on, but later discovers a clue that might help him win her back. The film explores the intricacies of their relationship, providing a unique blend of humor, romance, and self-discovery.
